# 🚀 Tesla Social Media Analytics Project (Excel)

## 🎯 Problem Statement
Tesla invests heavily in digital marketing across platforms, but lacks a **centralized system** to:
- Measure campaign performance  
- Identify high-performing content  
- Track ROI on ad spend  
- Understand follower growth patterns  

---

## 🎯 Objective
To build an **Excel-based Social Media Intelligence Dashboard** that:
- Analyzes engagement and content performance  
- Evaluates campaign effectiveness  
- Tracks platform-wise growth  
- Provides data-driven marketing recommendations  

---

## 📂 Dataset Description
The project uses three datasets:

### 1. Posts Data
- Individual Tesla posts across platforms  
- Includes likes, shares, comments, impressions, hashtags  

### 2. Engagement Summary
- Weekly aggregated performance metrics  
- Tracks platform-level trends  

### 3. Campaign Data
- Information about Tesla marketing campaigns  
- Includes ad spend, clicks, impressions, etc.  

---

## 🧹 Data Preprocessing
Before analysis, the data was cleaned and standardized:

- Removed duplicate posts  
- Standardized date formats and platform names  
- Formatted numeric columns (Likes, Reach, Impressions, Ad Spend)  
- Split hashtags into individual tags for better analysis  

---

## 📊 Analysis Performed

### 🔹 1. Engagement Analysis
- Calculated **Engagement Rate**:  
  (Likes + Shares + Comments) / Impressions  
- Identified **Top 10 posts** with highest engagement  
- Used Pivot Tables to analyze:
  - Engagement by content type & platform  
  - Average clicks per hashtag  

---

### 🔹 2. Platform Analysis
- Identified platform with highest engagement  
- Compared follower growth rates  
- Analyzed **Engagement vs Ad Spend**  
- Generated platform-wise recommendations  

---

### 🔹 3. Hashtag & Content Strategy
- Identified most frequently used hashtags  
- Compared performance across hashtags  
- Evaluated content type performance  
- Recommended platform-specific content strategies  

---

### 🔹 4. Campaign Effectiveness
- Calculated:
  - Total & average impressions, likes, clicks per campaign  
  - Engagement uplift during vs before campaigns  
- Evaluated:
  - ROI (Engagement vs Ad Spend)  
  - Campaign-driven follower growth  

---

### 🔹 5. Follower Retention & Loyalty
- Visualized weekly follower growth  
- Identified peak growth periods  
- Applied moving averages for trend analysis  
- Correlated ad spend with follower growth  

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Instagram shows the **highest average engagement rate**  
- Twitter and Instagram deliver **higher engagement even with lower ad spend**  
- High-performing hashtags include:
  - #EVRevolution
- Lower **Cost per Engagement (CPE)** leads to higher ROI - CyberTruckLaunch campaign had the highest ROI. 

---

## 💡 Recommendations

### 📌 Platform Focus
- Prioritize **Instagram and Twitter** for marketing efforts  
- These platforms provide **better engagement efficiency and ROI**

---

### 📌 Content Strategy by Platform
- **Instagram:** Story, Reels, Video  
- **Facebook:** Story, Image  
- **Twitter:** Story, Carousel  
- **YouTube:** Carousel, Image  

---

### 📌 Campaign Strategy
- Focus on campaigns with **low Cost per Engagement (CPE)**  
- The **Cybertruck Launch campaign** delivered the highest ROI  
- Optimize ad spend towards high-performing campaigns
